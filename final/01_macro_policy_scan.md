# Macro Policy & Timing Scan (Phase 11): 2029/2030 Deep-Tech Opportunity Landscape

## TL;DR
- The strongest, most defensible 2029/2030 windows sit at the intersection of the **AI-power buildout** (800VDC/MW-rack power electronics, liquid cooling, thermal metrology, LDES balance-of-system) and **advanced-packaging yield tooling** (hybrid-bonding metrology, chiplet thermal characterization) — each backed by dated, named policy/technical signals (NVIDIA 800VDC at Kyber/2027; ARPA-E COOLERCHIPS; DOE Long Duration Storage Shot; China 15th FYP "AI+" and East Data West Compute) and by named buyers (NVIDIA, Vertiv, Eaton, Schneider, Samsung, SK hynix, Onto Innovation, NRL).
- China's 15th Five-Year Plan (adopted March 12, 2026) and US policy (CHIPS metrology, SBIR/STTR reauthorization through FY2031 signed April 2026, FAR 6.302 sole-source) create parallel demand pulls, but the **export-control regime is volatile** (Federal Register 2026-00789 of Jan 15 2026; AI OVERWATCH Act advanced 42-2; Section 301 semiconductor tariffs scheduled for June 23 2027), so compute-adjacent and China-fab-equipment-facing clusters carry real risk.
- **Overhyped/crowded:** standalone humanoid robots, eVTOL airframes, diagnostic/radiology AI, generic AI dashboards. **Genuinely maturing by 2029/2030:** high-voltage DC power conversion, data-center liquid cooling, hybrid-bonding metrology/inspection, thermal-characterization instruments, LDES power-conversion/BOS, SiC rad-hard/high-temp electronics, and embodied-AI actuation/power subsystems (component layer, not whole robots).

## Key Findings

**1. The AI-power nexus is the single largest, best-sourced opportunity field.** The IEA "Energy and AI" Base Case (canonical: ~945 TWh data-center electricity by 2030 from 415 TWh in 2024) confirms the demand backdrop. Per the IEA, "China and the United States are the most significant regions for data centre electricity consumption growth, accounting for nearly 80% of global growth to 2030. Consumption increases by around 240 TWh (up 130%) in the United States…In China it increases by around 175 TWh (up 170%)" (FACT, Tier 1). The hardware inflection is dated and named: NVIDIA's 800VDC architecture reaches full-scale production with Kyber rack-scale systems in 2027, supporting 1 MW racks, with a named silicon/power-systems consortium (Infineon, STMicroelectronics, Texas Instruments, Navitas, Innoscience, ROHM, onsemi; Delta, Flex, LiteOn, Megmeet; Eaton, Schneider Electric, Vertiv) (FACT, company/consortium claim corroborated across DCD, eeNews, ST). This validates founder clusters C01–C03, C07, C18.

**2. China's 15th FYP reorganizes industrial policy around "AI+" and self-reliance.** Adopted by the NPC March 12, 2026, the plan drops the Made-in-China-2025 70% chip self-sufficiency target in favor of a deployment metric (core digital-economy value-added to 12.5% of GDP by 2030), targets R&D growth above 7%/yr, and elevates low-altitude economy, humanoid/embodied intelligence, advanced semiconductors, biomanufacturing, and nuclear (110 GW by 2030) (FACT, Tier 1/2: gov.cn, CSIS, China Briefing). Big Fund III (¥344B/$47.5B, established May 2024) channels capital to equipment, materials, and advanced packaging (FACT, Tier 3 triangulated; Nikkei/Fortune/SCMP).

**3. US non-dilutive funding rails are restored and deep.** SBIR/STTR reauthorized through FY2031 (S.3971, signed ~April 13 2026, ~$6B, adds a Strategic Breakthrough Allocation up to $30M/project) after a Sept 30 2025–April 2026 lapse (FACT, Tier 1: Congress.gov). DOE/ARPA-E programs and NIST CHIPS Metrology fund the exact subsystem layers the founder targets. FAR 6.302 sole-source is a live procurement path: NRL posted a Notice of Intent (N00173-25-RFI-MF18, July 28 2025) to sole-source an SSTR/FDTR thermal-characterization system from Laser Thermal Analysis, Inc. for wide/ultrawide-bandgap semiconductor metrology (FACT, Tier 1: SAM.gov).

**4. Export-control volatility is the dominant risk axis.** BIS Federal Register 2026-00789 (effective Jan 15 2026) moved H200/MI325X-class chips (TPP <21,000; DRAM BW <6,500 GB/s) from presumption of denial to case-by-case for China/Macau, with 25% tariff, ≤50% volume cap, and US third-party testing — but it is contested (House Foreign Affairs advanced the AI OVERWATCH Act 42-2; ~400,000 approved H200s reportedly frozen amid a State Dept review). Section 301 tariffs on Chinese legacy chips and SiC substrates are scheduled to rise from 0% to an announced rate on June 23 2027, stacking on an existing 50% tariff. Treat all compute-adjacent and China-fab-equipment-facing assumptions as provisional (FACT, Tier 1: Federal Register; Tier 2/3 triangulation).

**5. Reconciliations resolved:**
- **WSTS:** Use the most recent print. WSTS Spring 2026 (released June 2 2026): 2026 = $1.51T, +89.9% YoY; memory +249.5% to $803.9B and logic +37.3% to $411.4B, revised up from the Dec 2025 (Autumn 2025) forecast of $975.4B (+26.3%). Flag as a single revision, do not stack. Treat as market context only, not pain.
- **Data-center power:** Canonical 945 TWh (IEA Apr 2025 Base Case, 2024 baseline of 415 TWh). The IEA late-2025 update (~950 TWh from a 2025 baseline of 485 TWh) is the same body's refresh, not a conflicting third party.
- **US grid batteries:** 18.2 GW was the EIA Feb-2025 forecast; 15 GW actual 2025; 2026 = record 86 GW total additions, of which ~24.3 GW battery storage (EIA, Feb/Apr 2026).
- **MKS Instruments concentration:** top-ten customers = 32% of FY2024 net revenue (none >10%); do not use "top-5 41–49%."

## Details: Answers to the Six Research Questions

**Q1 — China policy priorities (2026–2030) creating startup opportunities.** 15th FYP (NPC, Mar 12 2026): AI+ as organizing logic; advanced semiconductors, robotics/embodied intelligence, biomanufacturing, quantum, 6G; low-altitude economy elevated (NDRC created a Low-Altitude Economy Development Division Dec 2024; two national civil-drone standards effective May 1 2026; MIIT HEIS humanoid standards released Feb 28 2026). Energy/grid/nuclear: non-fossil "ten-year doubling," 110 GW nuclear by 2030, CAP-1400 at-scale (SPIC ¥200B 2026 investment), AI+ Smart Energy pilots (Nov 2025). East Data West Compute (东数西算): 8 hub nodes/10 clusters, "national computing network," western renewable-powered AI-training hubs. NMPA "green channel"/Special Review for innovative devices (104 devices admitted in 2025; CMDE clinical-trial protocol pre-review issued Mar 13 2026; High-End Medical Device Priority Approval Catalog under Announcement No. 63 of 2025). Big Fund III (¥344B) for equipment/materials/packaging. (FACT/INFERENCE mix; Tier 1/2; high confidence on plan content, medium on which sub-sectors convert fastest.)

**Q2 — US policy priorities creating opportunities.** CHIPS-funded power-delivery/packaging R&D and NIST metrology (NIST CHIPS Metrology awarded Laser Thermal Analysis a Phase I, $289,831, Sept 17 2024); DOE grid modernization and Long Duration Storage Shot (90% cost cut by 2030 for 10+ hr; $349M demonstrations / $100M pilots); ARPA-E COOLERCHIPS (DOE press release "up to $42 million" at solicitation; "$40 million for 15 projects" at award; target "less than 5% of a typical data center's IT load"; ARPA-E's Peter de Bock: "we intend to have a proof of concept by the first half of 2026"; NREL Flatirons test site); SBIR/STTR through FY2031 with a new $30M Strategic Breakthrough track; FAR 6.302 sole-source and DIU/OT vehicles; FDA PCCP framework (final AI guidance Aug 2025) enabling iterative AI-enabled device software. (FACT, Tier 1; high confidence.)

**Q3 — China/US tensions creating opportunity/risk.** BIS 2026-00789 (case-by-case H200/MI325X, contested); AI OVERWATCH Act (advanced 42-2; would ban Blackwell-class to China 2 years and give Congress veto over chip-export licenses); 25% Section 232 chip tariff (Jan 14 2026 proclamation); Section 301 legacy-chip + SiC-substrate tariffs (0%→TBD on June 23 2027, atop existing 50%); Entity List exposure for China-fab-facing equipment; outbound-investment/Military-Civil Fusion dual-use exposure. Opportunity: import-substitution and "trusted-supply" plays inside each bloc; risk: any compute-adjacent or China-cleanroom-equipment cluster faces whipsaw. (FACT/INFERENCE; Tier 1/2; high confidence on rules, low on durability.)

**Q4 — Tailwind + control/regulatory risk mapping.** (a) Advanced packaging metrology/hybrid bonding: tailwind from HBM4/HBM4E ramp and Big Fund III; risk from Entity-List/SME controls and Section 301 if China-fab-facing. (b) AI-compute power/cooling: huge tailwind; risk if classified as compute-adjacent under future BIS expansion or if sold into China hyperscalers. (c) SiC substrates/devices: tailwind from 800VDC + EV; risk that Section 301 explicitly names SiC substrates. (d) Embodied-AI/low-altitude China-first: strong China tailwind; risk of US outbound-investment and dual-use exposure, and Chinese data/surveillance-standard lock-in. (e) Biomedical devices: NMPA green channel + FDA PCCP tailwind; risk of MCF/data rules. (INFERENCE; medium-high confidence.)

**Q5 — Technically mature by 2029/2030 vs too early.** Mature/feasible for small-team prototypes 2026–2028: 800VDC/±400VDC rack conversion and busways; active power-decoupling buffers; hybrid-bonding metrology/inspection (Onto Dragonfly G5 selected for HBM4; Applied/Besi); chiplet thermal characterization (SSTR/FDTR; NRL sole-source); 45°C liquid-cooling loops/CDUs; LDES power-conversion/BOS; SiC rad-hard/high-temp electronics; power-electronics reliability/qualification tooling; wearable electrochemical/microneedle biosensor subsystems. Too early/uncertain by 2029: full humanoid autonomy; certified passenger eVTOL at scale outside China; molten-salt-reactor commercial deployment (materials qualification is multi-year); two-phase immersion at data-center scale (ARPA-E expects 90% of COOLERCHIPS projects to miss their objectives). (INFERENCE; Tier 1/2 maturity signals; medium-high.)

**Q6 — Overhyped/crowded vs genuinely maturing.** Overhyped/crowded (market-size hype substituting for demand): standalone humanoid robots (per China's MIIT, "more than 140 humanoid robot manufacturers and more than 330 models in 2025"; Omdia counts >13,000 humanoids shipped globally in 2025, ~87–90% Chinese-made, with the Chinese government "publicly warn[ing] about the risk of a bubble" — fragmentation, not demand); eVTOL airframes; diagnostic/radiology AI (per FDA's AI-enabled device list through end-Dec 2025: 1,451 AI-enabled devices authorized since 1995, of which 1,104 are radiology — 76% of total); generic AI dashboards; broad "data-center TAM" pitches. Genuinely maturing: the power/thermal/packaging subsystem layers above. (INFERENCE; high confidence on the avoid-list given founder guardrails.)

## Recommendations
1. **Anchor 2026–2028 R&D pilots on the AI-power/thermal/packaging subsystem layer** (MC01, MC04, MC06, MC07, MC19, MC20) — densest dated signals, named buyers, small-team prototype feasibility. Pursue SBIR/STTR (funded through FY2031, including the new $30M Strategic Breakthrough track) and FAR 6.302 sole-source (NRL/Laser Thermal precedent) as non-dilutive customer-discovery rails. Target a demonstrable subsystem prototype before the NVIDIA Kyber/800VDC 2027 production inflection.
2. **Run China-first clusters (MC11, MC12) as parallel, ring-fenced tracks** given outbound-investment/MCF exposure; lead with components (actuators, powertrain/thermal, airspace subsystems), not whole systems, and design for credible US expansion.
3. **Stage gates / benchmarks that change the plan:**
   - If the **AI OVERWATCH Act passes** (restricting compute-adjacent), de-risk away from MC01/MC03/MC19 China exposure and pivot those to US/allied-only sales.
   - If the **Section 301 SiC-substrate tariff rate (effective June 23 2027)** lands high, accelerate domestic-supply plays in MC02/MC13.
   - If the **WSTS memory super-cycle reverses** in a later print, treat packaging-metrology timing (MC04/MC05) as less urgent and reweight toward power/cooling.
   - If **JEDEC HBM4E height relaxation** holds (pushing full hybrid-bonding adoption past ~2028), keep MC04 emphasis on inspection/metrology for microbump+HB transition rather than betting on full-HB process tooling.

## Caveats

- The NRL SSTR/FDTR action is a **Notice of Intent only (N00173-25-RFI-MF18, July 28 2025)**; the resulting award value/date is **unresolved** in SAM.gov/USAspending/FPDS as of June 2026.
