## Key Findings (corpus-construction outcomes)
1. **The 15th Five-Year Plan (2026–2030) is well-covered by primary sources.** The CPC Fourth Plenum Recommendations (adopted 2025-10-23) and the NPC-approved Outline (2026-03-12) are captured via official English full texts plus independent annotated translation (CSET) and analytical decoding (MERICS, Bruegel, CRS).
2. **Semiconductor policy triangulates across tiers.** Big Fund III (RMB 344 billion) primary/state confirmation (english.gov.cn, Caixin) is paired with the SMIC FY2025 audited HKEX filing (US$9,327 million revenue) and 2026 think-tank analysis (CSIS, Bruegel) — a clean Tier-1-anchored chain.
3. **Energy/grid data is the densest Tier-1 cluster:** NBS, NEA (via english.gov.cn), EIA, World Nuclear Association, and Reuters/SASAC together give 2025 capacity, generation, nuclear-approval, and grid-investment figures with verbatim numbers.
4. **Regulatory/standards/IP topics rely appropriately on official + specialist legal Tier 2.** NMPA, CNIPA, WIPO, CSET, World Bank, and major law-firm advisories (Pillsbury, Arnold & Porter) substantiate medtech pathways, IP statistics, standards strategy, export controls, and data rules.
5. **Analytical caution applied throughout:** market-size forecasts (low-altitude economy 1.5 trillion yuan; eVTOL projections) are explicitly labeled as forecasts/SPECULATION, not demand evidence; vendor/company claims are isolated to the SMIC filing (labeled company/audited filing).
