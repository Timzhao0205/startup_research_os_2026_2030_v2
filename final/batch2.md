# BATCH_02_US_POLICY_EXPORT — Source Corpus Batch (US Policy, Funding, Export Controls, Standards, Regulation)

## TL;DR
- **Logged 45 qualifying sources (US-001–US-045), 95.6% Tier 1/Tier 2**, spanning CHIPS implementation/NSTC/advanced packaging, BIS export controls (the Oct 7 2022 foundational rule through the Dec 2025 license-policy reversal), Treasury outbound investment, AI policy and NIST standards, energy/grid regulation, FDA medtech, biosecurity, SBIR/STTR, trade/Section 301, and federal procurement.
- **The single most important recency finding: US policy posture shifted materially in 2025–2026** — the Biden AI Diffusion Rule was rescinded (May 2025); Natcast's up-to-$7.4B NSTC award was voided and NIST took over operations (Aug 25 2025); SBIR/STTR authority lapsed (Oct 1 2025) then was reauthorized through FY2031 via S.3971 (signed Apr 13 2026); USTR concluded a Section 301 semiconductor action (effective Dec 23 2025); and BIS moved Nvidia H200/AMD MI325X exports to China from presumption-of-denial to case-by-case review (rule issued Jan 13 2026, effective Jan 15 2026, paired with a 25% import tariff).
- **The Tier mix clears the 75% quality gate with margin**; Tier 3 was used only twice, for triangulation of fast-moving items (Natcast facility cancellations; outbound-investment transaction mechanics).
