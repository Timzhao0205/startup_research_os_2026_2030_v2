# Customer Interview Script Template

## Rule

Do not pitch. Do not ask “Would you buy this?” Ask about current workflow, pain, budget, urgency, and alternatives.

## Opening

I am studying technical workflows in [domain]. I am not selling anything. I want to understand how this process works today, where it fails, and what high-priority improvements would matter.

## Questions

1. Walk me through the workflow from start to finish.
2. Where does the workflow fail most often?
3. What happens when it fails?
4. Who owns the consequence of that failure?
5. What is the current workaround?
6. What tools or vendors do you use today?
7. What do you dislike about the current solution?
8. How often does this problem occur?
9. How expensive is the problem in time, yield, reliability, safety, revenue, or capex?
10. Who owns the budget to fix it?
11. What has your organization already tried?
12. What would make a new solution credible enough to test?
13. What certifications, standards, reliability data, or validation would you require?
14. How long would procurement take?
15. What would make you reject a new vendor immediately?

## Scoring

Pain severity: 1–5
Pain frequency: 1–5
Budget clarity: 1–5
Urgency: 1–5
Current solution dissatisfaction: 1–5
Pilot willingness: 1–5
